Hobbies

My hobbies include Playing tabletop games such as settlers of Katan, Eldritch Horror, Love Letter and many more. I also enjoy playing tabletop RPGs the most popular of them being D&D.

In most of our D&D games I take the role of the DM. The players are split into the DM and the PCs. The PCs play inside the world of the DM. As a DM you take the role of the storyteller in a world where you put your player to fight monsters and tell epic tales of their adventures.

As a DM you are required to be prepared as sessions usually can last many hours and you have to be on the edge mentally so you can intrique your players into the world you created. Being the DM requires acting and a big ammount of quick calculation and decision making.
